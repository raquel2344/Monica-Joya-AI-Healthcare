# Module 4 – Understanding a Medical Test: The Sepsis ImmunoScore

## Purpose
This assignment required evaluating a real AI-based diagnostic tool. The paper examined the Sepsis ImmunoScore, the first AI-based diagnostic tool authorized by the FDA specifically to help detect sepsis. The report covered how the tool works, its clinical performance, and who it could benefit most.

## Key Takeaways
The Sepsis ImmunoScore analyzes 22 different patient measurements to determine whether a patient has or is developing sepsis within 24 hours. Understanding sensitivity and specificity is key when evaluating any diagnostic test. A high sensitivity means fewer sick patients go undetected, while high specificity means fewer false alarms on healthy individuals. The ImmunoScore performed well on both, but no test is perfect.

## Insights
This assignment highlighted how AI tools can bring together data points that would be overwhelming for a single clinician to monitor manually. In time-critical conditions like sepsis, where every minute counts, AI-assisted diagnostics can make a real difference in patient survival. It also reinforced the importance of understanding a tool's limitations before trusting its output.
