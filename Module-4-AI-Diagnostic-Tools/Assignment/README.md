# Module 4 – Understanding a Medical Test: The Sepsis ImmunoScore

## Purpose
I evaluated the Sepsis ImmunoScore, the first AI-based diagnostic tool authorized by the FDA specifically to help detect sepsis. My report covered how the tool works, how well it performs, and who it could benefit most.

## Key Takeaways
The Sepsis ImmunoScore analyzes 22 different patient measurements to determine whether a patient has or is developing sepsis within 24 hours. I learned that sensitivity and specificity are the two key measures for evaluating any diagnostic test. High sensitivity means fewer sick patients go undetected, while high specificity means fewer false alarms. The ImmunoScore scored well on both, but no test is perfect.

## Insights
What stood out to me most was how AI can bring together data points that would be overwhelming for a single clinician to track manually. In time-critical conditions like sepsis, where every minute matters, that kind of speed can save lives. This assignment also taught me to always ask about a tool's limitations before trusting its output.
