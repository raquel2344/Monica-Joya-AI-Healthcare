Exploring SDOH Data

Exploring how social determinants of health — housing stability, income, food access, neighborhood safety — can be incorporated into predictive AI models. This assignment examined how SDOH data changes a model's view of patient risk and why ignoring it produces biased outcomes.

Week 6 Discussion

Weekly discussion on how ZIP code, income level, and housing data can predict health outcomes better than many clinical biomarkers — and what that means for AI fairness and equity.

Module Reflection

"Your zip code should not predict your health outcomes — but it does. This module made me confront how deeply structural inequality is baked into the very data that AI healthcare models are trained on, and how much work it takes to even begin to correct for it."
