Learning to think clinically.

I came into this course thinking in data pipelines and model outputs, with very little sense of what it actually means to care for a patient. What I found was that AI in healthcare is not just about model architecture. It is about understanding why a nurse would distrust an algorithm, why data from underserved populations is systematically underrepresented, and why explainability is not optional when a patient's life is on the line.
