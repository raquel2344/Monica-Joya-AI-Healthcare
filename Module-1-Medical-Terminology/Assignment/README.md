# Module 1 – Medical Terminology Identification

## Purpose
This assignment focused on identifying and explaining ten medical terms drawn from three peer-reviewed articles on AI in healthcare. The goal was to build a working vocabulary of clinical and technical terms that are essential for understanding how AI tools are being applied in patient care.

## Key Takeaways
Medical terminology is the foundation for understanding AI applications in healthcare. Terms like electrocardiogram (ECG), structural heart disease, and aortic stenosis are not just clinical vocabulary. They represent the exact data types and conditions that AI systems are being trained to detect. Without understanding what these terms mean, it becomes difficult to evaluate whether an AI tool is doing its job well or producing misleading results.

## Insights
Working through this assignment made it clear that AI in healthcare is not a separate field from clinical medicine. The two are deeply connected, and anyone working with AI-based diagnostic or screening tools needs to understand the medical context behind the data these systems process.
