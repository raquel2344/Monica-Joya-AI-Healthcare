# Module 1 – Medical Terminology Identification

## Purpose
In this assignment, I identified and explained ten medical terms from three peer-reviewed articles on AI in healthcare. The goal was to build a working vocabulary of clinical and technical terms that come up when AI tools are applied to patient care.

## Key Takeaways
I learned that terms like electrocardiogram (ECG), structural heart disease, and aortic stenosis are not just clinical vocabulary. They represent the exact data types and conditions that AI systems are being trained to detect. If I do not understand what these terms mean, I cannot meaningfully evaluate whether an AI tool is producing reliable results or misleading ones.

## Insights
This assignment showed me that AI in healthcare is not a separate discipline from clinical medicine. The two are deeply connected, and working in this space requires understanding the medical context behind the data these systems process. It set the tone for everything that followed in the course.
